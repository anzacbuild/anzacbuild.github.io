Tips for aNZac Build

This section contains Tips and Guides to help you get the most out of aNZac Build as well as trouble troubleshooting and general instructions.

Navigation: If you are using a keyboard to control Kodi the arrow keys navigate up, down, left and right. The Enter Key is 'OK' and Backspace is 'BACK'. The Minus Key (-) is 'Volume Down' and the Plus Key (+) is 'Volume Up'. The Backslash Key (\) toggles between full screen and windowed mode.

Refresh Live TV Channel links and IPTV Guide info
To update the Live TV Channel links and IPTV Guide information navigate to 'Live TV' and press OK twice. You will see a notification on the top of the screen saying 'Merge Started' followed a short time later by another notification saying 'Merge Complete'. Press BACK to finish. The Live TV channel links and IPTV Guide information will now be updated.
Note: This process happens automatically every couple of days to keep the Live TV section updated but sometimes a manual update is required.
