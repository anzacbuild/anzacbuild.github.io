Install Live TV

This guide will show you how to install the PVR IPTV Simple Client add-on in aNZac Build and setup Live TV with the default channel list and TV guide. The PVR IPTV Simple Client enables both IPTV and Radio. By default New Zealand Free-view, Australian Free-view and New Zealand Radio Stations are installed.

Note: Some IPTV channels are Geo-blocked and cant be accessed from some locations without a VPN or similar.

Step A
Navigate to 'Live TV' and press OK (as pictured on the left)
Note: If you already have Tv Chanels displayed you don't need to do this. (as pictured right)

Step B
Navigate down to 'IPTV Install' and press OK

Step C
Navigate left to 'YES' and press OK

Step D
The PVR IPTV Simple Client will install. (as pictured on the left)
Now a completion message will be displayed. press OK (as pictured right)

Step E
press BACK

Step F
Navigate up to 'Live TV' and right to 'IPTV Merge: Click me t...' and click OK

Step G (Finished)
You will now see a message 'Merge Started'
Wait until you see the message 'Merge' Complete'. The default channel list will now load and you are finished.
