Add IPTV Subscription

This guide will show you how to install a Subscription Add-on and add its Channels and Program Guide to the Live TV section of aNZac Build.

The following is a list of Add-ons that are compatible with the Live IPTV section
New Zealand: TAB (no EPG), Sky Go, Sky Sport Now and Spark Sport
Australia: Binge, Foxtel Go, Foxtel Now, Kayo Sports and Optus Sport
Other: beIN Sports Connect, DStv Now and Rugby Pass (HK)

In the following example we will install the Sky Go Add-on and log in. Then we will add the live channel streams and guide to the Live IPTV section of aNZac Build.

Step A
Navigate to 'Subscriptions' and across to 'NZ Sky Go' and press OK

Step B
Navigate left to 'Yes' and press OK to install the NZ Sky Go Add-on.

Step C
Navigate to 'Login' and press OK. (Login should already be selected)

Step D
Enter your Sky Go account login email address and press OK on 'Done'.

Step E
Enter your Sky Go account password and press OK on 'Done'.

Step F
You are now logged in and can view live streams and on demand content provided by the Add-on.
Press BACK to Continue.

Step G
Navigate up to 'Live IPTV' and press OK twice

Step H
Navigate down to 'Playlists' and press OK

Step I
Navigate down to 'Add Playlist' and press OK

Step J
Navigate down to 'Add-on' and press OK

Step K
Navigate down to 'Sky Go' and press OK

Step L
Navigate left to 'YES' and press OK

Step M
Navigate down to 'Add-on' and press OK

Step N
Navigate down to 'Sky Go' and press OK

Step O
You will see 'Sky Go' is now in the list. press BACK to continue

Step P
Navigate DOWN to 'Run Merge' and press OK.
You will now see a notification 'Merge Started' followed shortly with another notification 'Merge Complete'
The channels and guide will now reload with the new information. Press BACK to finish.

Step Q
You have now successfully added the Sky Go Subscription channels to Live IPTV (as pictured left)
and also added the program information to the IPTV Guide (as pictured right)
