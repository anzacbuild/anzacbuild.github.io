Install aNZac Build


 Kodi 18 Leia  is available for a wide range of devices including Windows, Linux, MacOS, Android 6+, Raspberry Pi and iOS.

This page will walk you through installing aNZac Build 8 on a fresh install of  Kodi 18 Leia . The guide assumes Kodi 18 Leia is installed correctly on your device and your device is connected to the Internet.


Step A
Navigate to the 'settings cog' in the top left of the screen and press OK



Step B
Navigate to the 'File manager' on the top left of the screen and press OK



Step C
Navigate down to 'Add source' and press OK

Step D
Navigate to '<None>' and press OK

Step E
Type in 'https://anzacbuild.github.io/anzac' (do not type the quotation marks) then navigate to 'OK' and press OK

Step F
Navigate down to 'OK' and press OK
Note: if you get the error 'Unable to connect' (as pictured to the right) you have typed the text in Step E wrong or your device is not connected to the Internet. Select 'NO' and correct the error before continuing.

Step G
Press BACK to return to the main 'Settings' screen

Step H
Navigate right to 'Add-ons' and press OK

Step I
Navigate down to 'Install from zip file' and press OK

Step J
Navigate left to 'Settings' and press OK
Note: If this is not the screen you see now skip forward to Step O below.

Step K
Navigate to 'Unknown Sources' and press OK

Step L
Navigate left to 'Yes' and press OK

Step M
Press BACK to return to the 'Add-ons' screen

Step N
Navigate to 'Install from zip file' and press OK

Step O
Navigate to 'anzac' and press OK

Step P
Navigate down to 'plugin.program.anzactools.zip' and press OK

Step Q
Wait until the 'aNZac Build Tools' screen appears. (this might take a couple of minutes)
Read the DISCLAIMER: you alone are responsible for the content you choose to watch so please respect copyrights etc...
Navigate left to 'Accept' and press OK

Step R
Navigate to 'Continue' and press OK
(Continue should already be selected)

Step S
Navigate to 'Build Menu' and press OK
(Build Menu should already be selected)

Step T
Navigate down to '[18.7] aNZacBuild (v8.X.X)' (X.X is the version number) (7.7 at writing)
and press OK

Step U
Navigate down to 'Fresh Install' and press OK

Step V
Navigate left to 'Continue' and press OK

Step W
Wait for aNZac Build to Download. (pictured left)
Then wait for aNZac Build to Install. (pictured right)

Step X
Navigate to 'Force Close' and press OK
(Force Close should already be selected)

Step Y
Restart the Kodi app to complete the instillation. (on some devices this may happen automatically)
Note: It may take several minutes for the final updates to complete and on rear occasions the device may need to be restarted.
Congratulations aNZac Build should now be successfully installed.
